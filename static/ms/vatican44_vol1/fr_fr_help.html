Discriminating similar letters
Dalet (ד) Reish (ר)

Usually it is easy to distinguish between ד and ר in Vatican manuscript. the ר is round and drawn in one stroke while the vertical line of the ד usually leans to the left with a clear angle at the top right corner.

TL;DR - rounded top right corner - ר square corner - ד

רד
               

- קתדרה

- דבר ודבר

Sometimes the reish ר is square and looks like dalet (ד):

- חסר לב

Beit (ב) - Caf (כ)

The letters ב and כ can be easily distinguished. the כ is round and drawn in one stroke the ב is drawn in two main ways: or the vertical line connects to the lower horizontal line before its end, so there is a right “tail” or that the vertical line leans right and there is a clear angel with the lower horizontal line.

TL;DR - the ב has a sharp right bottom corner or “tail”, the כ has around lower right corner.

בכ
                 

- כ"ף בי"ת

- כקרבנות

Sometimes the ב becomes round or the כ becomes square and it becomes complicated to distinguish between them...

- בידם

- אשכון

Final M (ם) - Samech (ס)

It is hard to distinguish between final mem (ם) and smaech (ס) in the writing of Vatican scribe. The two letters have a great variety of square and round shapes. Distinguishing between them can be done by the place in the word and the probability of reading…

TL;DR - Decide by context
